Description:
Abstract:
Statewide Highways GIS Data Set from the Colorado Department of Transportation clipped to DWR Division 5
Purpose:
Features in this dataset represent segments of public highways that are maintained and under the jurisdiction of the Colorado Department of Transportation. These highways consist of Interstates, US Highways, and State Highways. Features are represented by polyline (linear) geographic shapes.
